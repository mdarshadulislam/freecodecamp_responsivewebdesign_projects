Delicious Mangoes From Rajshahi
Features

What Makes Them Special?

Rajshahi mangoes are famous for their sweet, creamy taste and lovely smell. They are usually medium to large in size.

Where Do They Come From?

Most Rajshahi mangoes come from Rajshahi district. Chapai Nawabganj is also a big place for growing mangoes.

Different Types of Rajshahi Mangoes

There are many kinds of Rajshahi mangoes, including Himsagar, Langra, Amrapali, Fazli, Gopalbhog, and Bari 4. Each one has its own special flavor.
